### RWA & Tokenization Explanation
**RWA (Real World Assets)**: Traditional assets with tangible or financial value (real estate, bonds, precious metals)
- Challenges: Low liquidity, high entry barriers, complex/costly transaction processes

**Tokenization**: Process of representing ownership rights and value of RWAs as digital tokens on blockchain
- Benefits: Fractional ownership, instant liquidity, smart contract automation, global investor access
- Result: Transforms traditional assets to digital economy speed and transparency
